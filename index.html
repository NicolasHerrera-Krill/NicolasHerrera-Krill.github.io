Videogame Development

ProbaUAM-VR

Investigation on VR games for the treatment and prevention of instability in older adults, at Autonomous University of Manizales.
Since 2021 I have developed multiple games on VR to help players with their different physical-therapeautical activities. They have been developed on Unity 3D for the SteamVR platform.

Cognitiv

Functional development of a serious video game platform in virtual reality to prevent and treat motor and cognitive impairment, for the Autonomous University of Manizales.
The games were developed in Unity for the Steam-VR platform. They were developed in 2023 to present the startup competition for PRO-Accelerator in Bizkaia.

Shadows In the Snow

(WIP) Retro 2D video game made as a personal project in Godot. It is an adaptation of several works by author H.P. Lovecraft, like Dreams in the Witch House or Nyarlathotep. It has been in development since 2020, and most of the art, software development, and design of the game is done by me.
